Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about my subscription service.

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time developer?

Great question! A senior developer costs over ‚Ç¨100,000 annually, plus social charges, holidays, training, equipment... And you still need to find one available! With me, you pay a fixed monthly rate only when you have projects. Need a break? Pause your subscription instantly and resume whenever you want.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

Once subscribed, you're able to add as many development requests to your queue as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one.

How fast will I receive my development work?

On average, most requests are completed in just two days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

Who are the developers?

That's me, Laurent Curau! You work directly with me, no team or intermediaries. I've been developing for over 10 years, based in Monaco. For very complex projects, I can bring in experts from my network if needed.

How do I request development work?

WebDev Pro offers a ton of flexibility in how you request development work using Trello. Some common ways clients request work is directly via Trello, sharing Google docs or wireframes, or even recording a brief Loom video (for those who prefer not to write their briefs out). Basically, if it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it's fair game.

What if I don't like the development work?

No problem! I'll revise the work as many times as needed until you're completely satisfied. It's included in the subscription.

Is there any development work you don't cover?

Yes, some things. I don't do: 3D modeling, animated videos (simple GIFs OK), heavy software or complex plugin development. I focus on web development: websites, web applications, APIs, integrations, automations.

How do I pause my subscription?

Very simple. You notify me by email or Trello and I immediately suspend your subscription. If you've used 21 days out of a 31-day cycle, you have 10 days of credit remaining that you can use when you resume, even months later. No justification needed.

What programs do you code in?

Most requests are coded using modern frameworks and technologies including React, Vue.js, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and various databases. We stay up-to-date with the latest web technologies and best practices.
