Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our web development subscription.

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time developer?

Good question! For starters, the annual cost of a full-time senior developer now exceeds €100 000, plus benefits (and good luck finding one available). Aside from that, you may not always have enough work to keep them busy at all times, so you're stuck paying for time you aren't able to utilise. With the monthly plan, you can pause and resume your subscription as often as you need to ensure you're only paying your developer when you have work available for them.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

Once subscribed, you're able to add as many development requests to your queue as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one.

How fast will I receive my development work?

On average, most requests are completed in just two days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

Who are the developers?

You might be surprised to hear this, but WebDev Pro is actually a one-person team. This means you'll work directly with me, founder of WebDev Pro. However, power-ups like additional experts are available for more complex projects when needed.

How do I request development work?

WebDev Pro offers a ton of flexibility in how you request development work using Trello. Some common ways clients request work is directly via Trello, sharing Google docs or wireframes, or even recording a brief Loom video (for those who prefer not to write their briefs out). Basically, if it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it's fair game.

What if I don't like the development work?

No worries! We'll continue to revise the development work until you're 100% satisfied.

Is there any development work you don't cover?

Absolutely. WebDev Pro does not cover the following development work: 3D modeling, animated videos (GIFS, etc. are OK), complex package or plugin development. Basically, if it doesn't involve coding websites or web applications, we probably don't cover it.

How do I pause my subscription?

We understand you may not have development work for us every month. Perhaps you've just finished a launch and need a breather. Billing cycles are based on 31 day period. Let's say you sign up and use the service for 21 days, and then decide you want to pause your subscription. This means that the billing cycle will be paused and you'll have 10 days of service remaining to be used anytime in the future.

What programs do you code in?

Most requests are coded using modern frameworks and technologies including React, Vue.js, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and various databases. We stay up-to-date with the latest web technologies and best practices.
